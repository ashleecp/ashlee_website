ABOUT ASHLEE

Hey There...

Ashlee’s lifelong passion for the arts and creating have led her to a career in design. She graduated from University of Central Florida in 2008 with a B.S. in Psychology. This, accompanied with studio art classes and a year at University of Colorado Denver for Digital Animation, has given Ashlee distinguishably remarkable talent in the advertising, marketing and graphic design field.


Beginning her career debut at a local advertising website, TeamDaveLogan.com, Ashlee quickly showed her design strength moving from digital and banner advertising design to the branding design of client companies advertising and marketing schema, as well as those of their clients. Her weekends and evenings are spent working with her freelance clients or over canvas-and-cocktails with girlfriends.


Areas of expertise include: logo design, brand design, marketing materials, email marketing, and graphic design.
